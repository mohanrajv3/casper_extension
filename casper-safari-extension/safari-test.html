🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Extension not visible: Check Safari → Preferences → Extensions
  • Autofill not working: Look for the CASPER button near password fields
  • Vault won't unlock: Verify your PIN is correct
  • No popup: Enable "Allow Unsigned Extensions" in Develop menu
